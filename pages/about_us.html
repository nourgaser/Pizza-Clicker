About us

After 8 years of serving bubbly, char-kissed pizza out of their home-made welded steel arch mobile wood fired ovens, PizzaMoto first brick and mortar home is now open!
Having looked for years for the perfect place to put down roots for their business, co-owners Dave Sclarow (executive chef) and Anna Viertel (general manager) found a turn of the century coal oven hidden behind a wall in a dark storefront at 338 Hamilton Ave, Brooklyn.
